Revolutionizing Electrician Training

ElectriGuide is an AI-powered mobile application that transforms how electricians learn and work. Our intelligent assistant provides step-by-step guidance through complex installations, analyzes work progress via photo verification, and delivers real-time feedback to ensure precision and safety.

Project Context

Problem

  • Major shortage of electricians: Demand is growing rapidly while experienced electricians are retiring faster than apprentices are entering the field.
  • Training bottleneck: Apprenticeship programs are resource-constrained (many students per instructor), limiting hands-on mentorship.
  • Emerging-tech skills gap: New installations (EV chargers, solar, data centers) require upskilling beyond traditional wiring knowledge.

Target Users

  1. Apprenticeship programs — vocational schools, unions, non-union contractors expanding cohorts but limited by instructor capacity.
  2. Field electricians — journeymen and techs needing just-in-time guidance for unfamiliar, high-tech installs.

Use Cases

  • Scalable lab training: Replace one-on-one bench supervision with guided exercises and remote instructor dashboards.
  • Just-in-time field support: Mobile step-by-step wiring instructions with camera verification for solar inverters, EV chargers, and data-center circuits.
